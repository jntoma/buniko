what is kombucha exactly?

Kombucha is a fermented tea that's been around for centuries. It's fizzy and can be flavored in many different ways!

is kombucha healthy for you?

Kombucha has many health benefits. Some of them are similar to a tea's, but others are entirely its own. For example, kombucha can be a source of probiotics, antioxidants, and could kill bacteria! It may also reduce the risk of heart disease.

what's the process of making kombucha like?

Kombucha is brewed over time starting with a piece of fermented yeast called a scoby. Reach out to us to find out how to brew your own kombucha!

can you drink too much kombucha?

Too much of anything can be a bad thing. Some people find that kombucha can make their bowels particularly active. Each person is different, but it's important to drink in moderation.

what's the difference between this kombucha and one that they sell in stores?

This kombucha is homebrewed with local ingredients and lots of love! Other than that, our processes are similar and similarly clean and safe.

can i make my own kombucha?

Sure! All you need is some jars, a scoby, and lots of time.
