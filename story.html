Our Story

the story of buni ko begins in Romania, in the late 60s, when a girl was born

raised on farmland, this girl, named after the lilies that grew in her front garden, grew into a young woman inextricably tied to nature

the woman grew older and eventually moved to america. here, in the suburbs of Michigan, her farmland seemed so out of reach. but bit by bit she began pulling it across to her from overseas

planting a cherry tree from her sister's garden, tomato plants from the kroger around the corner until her backyard transformed
