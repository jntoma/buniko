Our Story

The story of buni ko begins in Romania where Crina, the founder of buni ko, was born.

She was named for the lilies in the garden

of her childhood home.

Crina carried a love for nature, even when she moved to the less wild suburbs of Michigan.

She brought seeds to life and helped flowers bloom. She started making kombucha for herself in 2017 as she brought more nature into her life.

But nature gives to all, she thought, and so should she. So in 2020, she began buni ko to share her love for the earth with others.
